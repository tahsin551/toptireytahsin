About TopTire

Where the brand name comes from — and where it's going.

TopTire started with a simple idea: clothes should be able to take a hit and keep going, the same way a good tire does. No delicate fabrics, no "dry clean only" — just heavyweight cotton and honest cuts built for daily life in Chattogram.

We design every piece around traction: staying grounded, moving forward, and holding up under pressure. That's why our tags read like tire specs, and our tread-stripe is stitched into every drop.

We keep the buying side just as straightforward as the clothes — order online, then pay the way that actually works for you: cash when it arrives, or mobile banking if that's easier.
